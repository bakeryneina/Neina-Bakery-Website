About

Where tradition meets passion in every bite

Crafted with Care

At NEINA, we believe that great bread starts with time, patience, and quality ingredients. Our sourdough is naturally fermented using a centuries-old technique, creating a unique flavor and texture that you won't find anywhere else.

Located in the heart of Nara, we invite you to slow down, savor the moment, and enjoy the simple pleasures of artisan bread and thoughtfully prepared food.

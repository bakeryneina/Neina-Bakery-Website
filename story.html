Nara

We are proud to call Nara our home. With its ancient temples, friendly deer, and serene atmosphere, this city has a unique rhythm that inspires our baking.

Our bakery is situated on Mochiidono Street. Being part of this community means everything to us. We strive to honor the local culture by creating a gathering place that feels both modern and timeless, much like Nara itself.

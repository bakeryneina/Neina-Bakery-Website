Our Story

A journey of passion, tradition, and the spirit of Nara

The Shop

NEINA was born from a simple desire: to bring the authentic taste of artisan sourdough to Nara. Located in the historic "Yume Cube" complex, our cozy bakery is a place where locals and visitors alike can slow down and enjoy the simple pleasures of life.

The name NEINA represents our commitment to natural, wholesome ingredients. We designed our space to be warm and invitingâ€”a small haven where the aroma of freshly baked bread greets you at the door.

Nara

We are proud to call Nara our home. With its ancient temples, friendly deer, and serene atmosphere, this city has a unique rhythm that inspires our baking.

Our bakery is situated on Mochiidono Street, one of the oldest shopping streets in Nara. Being part of this community means everything to us. We strive to honor the local culture by creating a gathering place that feels both modern and timeless, much like Nara itself.
